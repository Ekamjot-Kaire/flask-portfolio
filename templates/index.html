basics of rollerskating

  1. roller skating is all about shifting your weight while maintainting balance
  2. for example, when moving forward, you dont place your feet in front of each other like you would while walking; instead you shift your weight from left to right to gain momentum
  3. many parts of roller skating, like spinning, turning, jumping, etc, is all about learning where to keep your weight
